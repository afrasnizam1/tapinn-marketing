About Us
TapInn was born out of a simple idea: making real-world connections and spontaneous meetups easy and enjoyable. We believe that the best moments in life happen when friends, old and new, can get together effortlessly, without the stress of endless group chats and planning.
Our team is passionate about technology, community, and creating delightful user experiences. TapInn is designed for everyone—whether you're organizing a big event, looking for new friends with shared interests, or just want to grab coffee with someone nearby.

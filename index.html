Frequently Asked Questions

Is TapInn free to use?
Yes! TapInn is free to download and use for everyone.
Do my friends need the app to get invites?
No. You can invite anyone using their phone number—they’ll get a text with your event details.
What platforms is TapInn available on?
TapInn will be available on both iOS and Android at launch.
How do reliability scores work?
Reliability is earned by showing up! Frequent cancellations or no-shows lower your score, while attending events keeps it high.
